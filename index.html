About

Deepak specializes in cloud security posture management, application security, and secrets governance — the layer between fast-moving engineering teams and the controls that keep them safe. His work spans onboarding multi-cloud accounts into CSPM platforms, triaging SAST/SCA findings with the developers who fix them, and running identity programs that hold up under audit.

He's drawn to problems where security has to earn its place in the pipeline rather than gate it — integrating secrets managers into CI/CD instead of leaving credentials hardcoded, mapping identity providers into vaults and posture tools instead of running parallel silos, automating the alerting nobody wants to build by hand.
